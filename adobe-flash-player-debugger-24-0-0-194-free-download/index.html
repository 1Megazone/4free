Adobe Flash Player Debugger 24.0.0.194 Review

Flash Player Debugger is a handy utility for the developers when it comes to finding out Flash and Flex errors. You can also call it a troubleshooting tool for Flex and Flash Projects. Most of you have used Adobe Flash Player, which is most of the time required for playing Flash media on the browsers, it is the debugger version of Flash Player to collect and fix the errors during the development of Flash Content. It is not recommended for home use as it is heavier on system resources as compared to the Adobe Flash Player and may result in slow playback of Flash Content.

The debugger is used to collect all the statistics during the playback so to find out any errors and other issues with the flash. This debugger comes in two modes i.e. ActiveX Control for running in the browser (Projector) and other is the standalone version for using outside the browser in case of flash development. It is used in collaboration with Flash Builder and other such applications.
